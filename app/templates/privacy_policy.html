Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, make a booking, or specifically provide details for energy calculations. This may include:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.
  • Energy usage data and household details for our calculation tools.
  • Booking details and service preferences.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • Process your bookings and send related communications.
  • Generate personalized energy and carbon footprint reports.
  • Send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support messages.

3. Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized processing and against accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can manage most of your personal details directly through your account profile.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
